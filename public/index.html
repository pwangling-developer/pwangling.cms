P W Angling is situated to the south of Norwich city centre beside the A146 ring road, minutes away from the A47/A146 interchange, ideally located for local and visiting anglers with ample parking on site.

Tackle from a wide range of manufacturers including Preston Innovations, Korum, Avid Carp, Middy, Shimano, Korda, Maver and many more.

Large range of ready to fish bait, pellets, meat, corn, shelf life and frozen boilies from Sonubaits, Dynamite, Mainline, Sensas & Ringers amongst others.

Fresh lugworm available to order.

Fresh maggots, casters turned on the premises and worms.

Full range of frozen baits for sea fishing and pike fishing.

Whether you're a specialist angler, a match angler, a sea angler, a pleasure angler or a beginner P W Angling aims to meet all your angling needs.
